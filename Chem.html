?? The Language of Chemistry

1. Symbols of Elements ??
H ? Hydrogen
O ? Oxygen
Na ? Sodium (Latin: Natrium)
Fe ? Iron (Latin: Ferrum)
Cu ? Copper (Latin: Cuprum)
2. Valency ??

Valency = Combining power of an element.

  • H = 1 (Monovalent)
  • O = 2 (Divalent)
  • Al = 3 (Trivalent)
  • C = 4 (Tetravalent)
3. Formula Writing (Criss-Cross Method) ??
Na1+ + Cl1- ? NaCl
Ca2+ + Cl1- ? CaCl2
Al3+ + O2- ? Al2O3
4. Percentage Composition ??

Formula: (Mass of element / Molar mass) × 100

NH4NO3 ? N: 35%, H: 5%, O: 60%
